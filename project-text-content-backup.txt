1. ALLUEL (Original Version)
================================================================================

TITLE: Alluel
TAGS: Branding, Web

DESCRIPTION:
Brand identity and website redesign for a New York logistics company specializing in freight, moves, and furniture installations. The project encompassed a complete visual overhaul, from logo design to a fully responsive website.

DETAILS:
- Client: Alluel
- Year: 2024
- Services: Brand Identity, Web Design, Development
- Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS, JavaScript
- Live URL: Visit Site →

THE CHALLENGE:
Alluel needed a modern brand identity that would set them apart in the competitive New York logistics market. The existing branding felt outdated and didn't communicate the company's commitment to quality and reliability. The challenge was to create a visual system that felt both professional and approachable.

THE SOLUTION:
We developed a clean, bold brand identity centered around a custom wordmark and a vibrant blue color palette. The website was built with a focus on user experience, featuring clear service descriptions, an intuitive quote request flow, and responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices.

DETAILS:
- Client: Cannabis Oceania
- Year: 2023
- Services: Web Design, Data Visualization, Editorial Design
- Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, D3.js
- Live URL: Visit Site →

5. PHILANTHROPIC AGENDA
================================================================================

TITLE: Philanthropic Agenda
TAGS: Editorial Design

DESCRIPTION:
Trag Foundation published the Philanthropic Agenda, the first document of its kind to provide an overview of the key regulatory areas that need to be improved. This comprehensive publication required thoughtful editorial design to present complex policy information in an accessible and visually engaging format.

DETAILS:
- Client: Trag Foundation
- Year: 2023
- Services: Editorial Design, Layout, Print Design
- Tools: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop
- Live URL: Visit Site →

THE CHALLENGE:
The Philanthropic Agenda contained dense regulatory and policy content that needed to be presented in a way that was both authoritative and readable. The challenge was to create a design system that could handle complex data, charts, and lengthy text while maintaining visual interest throughout the 100+ page document.

THE SOLUTION:
We designed a clean editorial system with a strong typographic hierarchy, strategic use of color coding for different regulatory areas, and custom infographics to visualize complex data. Pull quotes and sidebar elements break up the text, while a consistent grid system ensures readability across all sections.

6. PROHIBITION PARTNERS LIVE
================================================================================

TITLE: Prohibition Partners LIVE
TAGS: Video Editing

DESCRIPTION:
Video editing and motion graphics for Prohibition Partners, a leading cannabis industry intelligence firm. The project involved creating dynamic video content for their LIVE series, combining data visualization with engaging editorial storytelling.

DETAILS:
- Client: Prohibition Partners
- Year: 2023
- Services: Video Editing, Motion Graphics, Data Visualization
- Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator
- Live URL: Visit Site →

THE CHALLENGE:
Prohibition Partners needed to transform complex cannabis market data and industry insights into compelling video content for their LIVE broadcast series. The content had to be both informative for industry professionals and visually engaging enough to maintain viewer attention across lengthy presentations.

THE SOLUTION:
We developed a motion graphics system with animated data visualizations, lower thirds, and transition sequences that maintained brand consistency throughout the series. Custom chart animations brought market data to life, while a clean editorial approach to video editing ensured the content remained professional and authoritative.

7. STADION SHOPPING CENTER
================================================================================

TITLE: Stadion Shopping Center
TAGS: Social Media

DESCRIPTION:
Social media content creation and strategy for Stadion Shopping Center, a major retail destination. The project encompassed content planning, graphic design, and campaign management across multiple social platforms.

DETAILS:
- Client: Stadion Shopping Center
- Year: 2024
- Services: Social Media Management, Content Creation, Graphic Design
- Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, Meta Business Suite
- Live URL: Visit Site →

THE CHALLENGE:
Stadion Shopping Center needed a cohesive social media presence that could promote diverse tenants, seasonal campaigns, and events while maintaining a unified brand voice. The challenge was creating a high volume of visually consistent content that would drive foot traffic and engagement across Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.

THE SOLUTION:
We established a comprehensive content system with templated post formats for different content types — tenant spotlights, event promotions, seasonal campaigns, and lifestyle content. A consistent color palette and typography system ensured brand recognition, while varied content formats (carousels, reels, stories) maximized engagement across platforms.
