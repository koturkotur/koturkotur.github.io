Cannabis Oceania

Web Editorial Design

Data visualization and editorial design for Cannabis Oceania, providing market insights for the Asia-Pacific cannabis industry. The project combined web design with editorial content to create a comprehensive digital publication.

The Challenge

Cannabis Oceania needed to present complex market data and regulatory information for the Asia-Pacific cannabis industry in a way that was both comprehensive and accessible to a diverse international audience. The content spanned multiple countries with different regulatory frameworks, market sizes, and growth trajectories.

The Solution

We created an interactive web publication with custom data visualizations that allow users to explore market data by country and category. The editorial design uses a clean, data-forward approach with interactive charts, comparative tables, and narrative sections that contextualize the numbers. Responsive design ensures the content is accessible on all devices.
